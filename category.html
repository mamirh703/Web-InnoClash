CATEGORY A

Students Innovators

Eligibility Requirements:

  • Must be currently enrolled in a recognized school, college, or university
  • Max 5 members per team
  • Must present an original innovation (product, service, or system)
  • Idea can be at concept, prototype, or early development stage
  • Must address a real-world problem (e.g., sustainability, education, health, tech)

First Place: RM800

Second Place: RM500

Third Place: RM300

CATEGORY B

Open / Professional Innovators

Eligibility Requirements:

  • Open to individuals, startups, researchers, or companies
  • No age or academic restrictions
  • Teams allowed (max 7 members)
  • Must present a functional prototype, product, or validated concept
  • Should demonstrate market potential or real-world application
  • Preferably includes testing, user validation, or pilot results

First Place: RM1300

Second Place: RM1000

Third Place: RM800
